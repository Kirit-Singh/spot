Methods & provenance

    
  

  

    
1

      
Data source

      
≈396,000 non-targeting-control CD4 T cells from the Marson genome-scale CRISPRi perturb-seq (primary human CD4); 4 donors × Rest / Stim 8 hr / Stim 48 hr. Public on the CZI Virtual Cells Platform and mirrored to Hugging Face at a pinned revision (SHA-256 verified). A 40,000-cell subsample of the embedding is shown here.

    

    
2

      
Embedding & clustering

      

    

    
3

      
Program scores

      
Every cell carries continuous RNA panel-minus-control scores — score_genes panel mean minus an expression-bin-matched control mean (25 bins, ctrl_size 50, fixed seed). 12 programs span three families (differentiation, functional, sensitivity), defined from the Masopust et al. nomenclature. These are continuous scores, not cell-type calls.

    

    
4

      
Activation sensitivity lane

      
Activation is a known confound for the Treg-like and CD4 CTL-like programs. An activation-adjusted CD4 CTL-like score (cd4_ctl_like_score_actadj) is reported alongside the raw score as a descriptive sensitivity check.
